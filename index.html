What is Crate?

Crate is an NFT-based music platform that provides master-quality streaming, an integrated marketplace, and a powerful blockchain to verify every transaction. Artists can mint their music into NFTs for free. And music lovers can buy and sell authenticated editions of their favorite records. And it’s all coded into the blockchain. Oh, and the majority of the revenue goes directly to the artist. So kinda like Apple Music or Bandcamp, but the sound quality is better, we pay artists better, and your purchases retain value on the marketplace.
