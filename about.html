About Us

At Crate we understand that music related tech companies haven’t exactly treated the artist community respectfully (*cough* *cough* Spotify).

So let’s start by saying we are a tech related music company, not the other way around. We are small; founded by 3 musicians who are firmly planted in the mission of treating artists and listeners with the respect and consideration they deserve.

NFTs are new, inaccessible, and, admittedly, a little scary. We would like to change that. On Crate, artists work directly with us to understand the benefits of our marketplace and the value of their music as NFTs. Please contact us directly with any questions.
