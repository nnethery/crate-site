Let’s talk about money...


How much do I make?

As a Crate artist you get paid for each initial sale of your work. You also get paid for each resale of your work. This is all possible with our smart contracts. So, you don’t have to worry about claiming anything, tracking anything, or reporting anything at the end of the year. We’ve got you covered.

We also pay you more than any other platform and yes, that's better than Bandcamp. To get down to literal numbers, you make 95% of the initial sale, and 10% of each sale there after.

How much do I pay?

It is absolutely free free to mint NFTs with us! We pay for everyone's accounts on the blockchain (artists and listeners), and the buyer of your work pays the gas fees.
