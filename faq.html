How does it work?

Using smart contracts, Crate’s technology automatically distributes the predetermined royalty amounts to the proper recipients, taking out the middleman and leaving more revenue for the artists. And since smart contracts will track every sale and re-sale of a record, for the first time ever, artists will receive proper royalties every time their music is sold, whether it is on the initial purchase, or the 100th re-sale.

How much does it cost?

It is free for artists to mint their NFTs, and we pay for all user account fees. The only cost is the music and the gas fees when purchasing an artist’s record.

How do I buy music on the marketplace?

Everything is available for purchase in USD just like it would be at any other store. At purchase you can see your wallet ID and the smart contracts we use to ensure our artists get paid accordingly!

I am an artist. How do I get my music on crate?

We’d be glad to help. Most artist info is available on our artists page. If we missed something, please contact us here and we’ll be in touch as soon as possible!

I want to buy my favorite artists’ music on crate.

We are currently only able to accept records and projects from independent artists who own the rights to their music. However, we intend to work with bigger artists in the future so you can listen to all your favorite music in master quality.
