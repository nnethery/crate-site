FAQ

How does it work?

Artists contact us to release their music as NFTs. We then work with the artist to mint and price their work, and release it on our marketplace for purchase and listening. Using smart contracts, Crate automatically distributes royalties to the proper parties when a record is purchased. Upon purchase of a record, listeners will be able to hear their record collection in master-quality audio, and play their collection offline, as well. A listener can also decide to sell something from their collection, and get paid for their resale. Smart contracts will track every re-sale of a record and distribute royalties to the artist and the reseller.

How much does it cost?

It is free for artists to mint their NFTs, and we pay for all user account fees. The only time you pay is when purchasing an artist’s record.

How do I buy music on the marketplace?

Everything is available for purchase in USD just like it would be at any other store. At purchase you can see your wallet ID and the smart contracts we use to ensure our artists get paid accordingly!

I am an artist. How do I get my music on crate?

We’d be glad to help. Most artist info is available on our artists page. If we missed something, please contact us here and we’ll be in touch as soon as possible!

I want to buy my favorite artists’ music on crate.

We are currently only able to accept records and projects from independent artists who own the rights to their music. However, we intend to work with bigger artists in the future so you can listen to all your favorite music in master quality.
